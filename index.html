Little about these demons

My name is Ginger. I am a 2 years old Siamese mixed cat. My hobby is meowing during my mom' sleeping time until she gets up and chases me

My name is Fish sauce. I am a 9 years old Calico chubby cat. My hobby is running out of our house as fast as I can so mom will play tag with me.

This are my 2 little demons. They are annoying for sure but I do love them with all my heart.
